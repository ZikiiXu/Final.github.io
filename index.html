Artist Statement

One thing I love the most about arts is it’s possibilities. There are no boundaries in arts. It is the language that can be used in world wild. It is also the language that can either contains verise meaning, or no meaning, but people still understand. People often consider theater as the western theater, but I think theater is for all. I want to take the old traditional Chinese stories, and make new adaptation that in some way connect to our modern world. Because they are so amazing and everyone deserved to hear and watch, and feel them. It is my goal to use the arts as the bridge, to connect the cultures and creatures together. From my work of art, I wish more people can start to understand the benefit and the joy that the arts can bring into our live.




Journal

I am from China, and I spend my Pre-k to 9th grade in China, which means the Chinese education system is rooted in my head. One thing I know is that we almost study all day and don’t have a lots of time to play, and we don’t have club or things like that. So when I first walk into the Cafe, which is the place for the children to stay and play, I was a little shocked and nervous. Even though, I been live in NY for few years already, but it is still hard for me to acknowledge the community from the neighborhood. The community is mix race, and I think it is pretty cloudy and loud, and with large population. The after programs are kind of chill and the stuffs are just there watching and regulating some inappropriate things. Right before the class, the stuffs and the teachers will gather the students. Some of the students are super behave, however, some of them are a little hard to control. The stuffs are very helpful and easy to connect with. They are willing to help me out. I am having hard time to connect with the kid. First, I think I don’t belong there yet, and I am not the lead teaching artist, so I am still a bit of scared to take the role. I also still didn’t get use to the environment yet. Also I look different and young, I feel the distance between us. That also make me feel apart. It might be hard for me to really connect with them in this short amount of the time. But I think by talking to them more and participate with them more, things will get easier. Also, by showing some of what I know, I think they will respect me more, which will make things easier for me when I actually teach the class at the end of the semester.
